### **🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)**



#### ✅ General Safety Tips



&#x09;✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

&#x09;✔️ Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages—even if they look official

&#x09;✔️ Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number before responding

&#x09;✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email

&#x09;✔️ Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software

&#x09;✔️ Be mindful of what you share online—avoid posting personal details like phone number, address, or college ID

&#x09;✔️ Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store

&#x09;✔️ Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers



#### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips



&#x09;✔️ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank

&#x09;✔️ Double-check UPI IDs, QR codes, and payment requests before sending money

&#x09;✔️ Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN—only payments do

&#x09;✔️ Enable transaction alerts (SMS/email) to monitor all bank activities



#### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime



&#x09;📌 Report incidents at: National Cyber Crime Portal – https://cybercrime.gov.in

&#x09;📞 Helpline Number: 1930 (for immediate assistance in financial fraud cases)
