# **🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)**



### ✅ General Safety Tips



      ✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

      ✔️ Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages—even if they look official

      ✔️ Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number before responding

      ✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email

      ✔️ Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software

      ✔️ Be mindful of what you share online—avoid posting personal details like phone number, address, or college ID

      ✔️ Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store

      ✔️ Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers



### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips



      ✔️ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank

      ✔️ Double-check UPI IDs, QR codes, and payment requests before sending money

      ✔️ Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN—only payments do

      ✔️ Enable transaction alerts (SMS/email) to monitor all bank activities



### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime



      📌 Report incidents at: National Cyber Crime Portal – https://cybercrime.gov.in

      📞 Helpline Number: 1930 (for immediate assistance in financial fraud cases)
