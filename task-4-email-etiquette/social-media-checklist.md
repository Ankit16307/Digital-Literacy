## ***Do’s***



✔️ Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate

✔️ Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings

✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

✔️ Be respectful and kind in comments and messages

✔️ Use social media for learning, networking, and positive engagement



## ***Don’ts***



❌ Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly

❌ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments

❌ Don’t post offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content

❌ Don’t believe or spread unverified news or rumors

❌ Don’t spend excessive time on social media at the cost of studies
